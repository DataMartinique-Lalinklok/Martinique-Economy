#Data Analysis About Martinican Economy
Martinique represents an interesting case for study for the relation between mass energy economics balance for the simplification.
Martinique does not have much natural ressources for modern industry. Current technical system is based on key technology including rare earth mineral, oil and gas.
Can a island with few ressources qnd isolated perform a peaceful simplification? Can basic standards of living be maintain to a sufficient level for the population? Can the economics engine be redirected to regeneration of ecosytem? These questions are not trivial and I do not have the answers. Unfortunately for my peace of mind.
This simplified economy analysis is based on import-export Jane Jacobs framework and the basis of ecology economy. It aims to picture the current states of martinican economies, and the ripples from the past and its potential furture.
