#Data Analysis About Martinican Economy

Martinique represents an interesting case for study for the relation between mass energy economics balance for the simplification.
Martinique does not have much natural ressources for modern industry. Current technical system is based on key technology including rare earth minerals, oil and gas.

Can a island with few ressources and isolated perform a peaceful simplification? Can basic standards of living be maintain to a sufficient level for the population? Can the economics engine be redirected to regeneration of ecosytem? These questions are not trivial and I do not have the answers.

This simplified economy analysis is based on import-export Jane Jacobs framework and the basis of ecology economy. It aims to picture the current states of martinican economies, and the ripples from the past and its potential furture. 

Feel free to download data visualization to have a better understanding of the situation.
